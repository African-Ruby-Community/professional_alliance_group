Michaelle Kubwimana

CEO & Founder at Kawah Coffee Ltd | CEO & Founder at Rwanda Business UK Ltd

As a passionate entrepreneur with a deep love for coffee and a commitment to community development, I have dedicated my career to creating meaningful impact both locally in Rwanda and globally. Having lived in 10+ countries since my early years and speaking fluently 6 languages, I want to create a bridge across continents. My journey began with a vision to harness the potential of our family’s rich coffee culture and turn it into a force for positive change by adding value at the source.

Founder & CEO of Kawah Coffee

Kawah Coffee is more than just a business; it is a movement to uplift and empower Rwandan coffee Farmers and especially women. At Kawah Coffee, we are committed to producing high-quality, ethically sourced coffee that not only delights the senses but also supports sustainable farming practices. Through our direct trade model, starting from our own family coffee farm, we ensure that farmers receive fair compensation, enabling them to invest in their communities and improve their livelihoods. We aim to have them celebrated by each person around the globe that will taste our Specialty coffee.

Our impact is seen in the thriving communities of coffee farmers who now not only just pick coffee cherries but are part of a fully farm-to-cup Specialty coffee brand, adding value onsite.

Founder and CEO at Rwanda Business UK

In addition to my work with Kawah Coffee, I founded Rwanda Business UK, a platform designed to bridge the gap between Rwandan and UK entrepreneurs and investors, promoting trade and investment between the two nations. Our mission is to foster economic growth and collaboration by creating opportunities for investment, trade, and knowledge exchange.

Rwanda Business UK serves as a hub for networking, resources, and support, making it easier for businesses from both countries to connect and thrive. Through this initiative, we are not only strengthening the economic ties between Rwanda and the UK but also promoting Rwanda as a dynamic and attractive destination for investment.

My Vision

My work is driven by a vision of a prosperous, interconnected world where businesses are a force for good. I believe in the power of entrepreneurship to create lasting change and am dedicated to building a legacy of empowerment, sustainability, and community.

Let’s connect and explore how we can collaborate to make a positive impact. Whether you are a fellow coffee enthusiast, an entrepreneur, or an investor, I would love to hear from you!
