CAA and Finplus Group: Providing Digital Financial Services Expansion

Through CAA (Caribbean American Alliance) is Finplus Group is undertaking a comprehensive IT transformation for a regional financial services provider in the Caribbean. Their role encompasses a wide range of activities to modernize the financial infrastructure and enhance service delivery across multiple islands.

Specifically, Finplus Group is responsible for:

  • Upgrading Core Financial Systems: They are tasked with implementing a new core financial platform to serve as the foundation of the modernized infrastructure.

  • Developing Interconnected Digital Applications: Finplus is creating multiple digital applications, each customized to meet the specific regulatory and user requirements of different island jurisdictions.
  • Enabling a Cohesive Financial Ecosystem: A key part of their work is to ensure these various applications can communicate with one another using standardized APIs. This will create a unified, region-wide financial ecosystem that supports cross-island transactions, streamlined compliance, and data sharing.
  • Implementing a Multi-Phased Project: The initiative is being carried out in several stages, which includes:
    • Enhancing a digital wallet solution.
    • Integrating digital currency and remittance services.
    • Deploying new ATMs.
    • Enabling card-based payments.
